The teacher of the class was an overly critical man named Reuben, who was a semester-long substitute for the real teacher.

Over the course of the semester, Reuben started to get a tad rude with his overly harsh critiques of our drawings.

At one point, Reuben wrote, "We're halfway through the semester, time to shit or get off the pot" on the back of one of my drawings.
